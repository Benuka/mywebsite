Nb-IoT Hackathon

by: Dialog Ideamart

Dialog Axiata PLC, Sri Lanka’s premier connectivity provider, in collaboration with Ideamart, hosted Sri Lanka’s first NB-IoT Hackathon (NB-IoT: Narrowband Internet of Things). The hackathon was carried out in two phases; phase one being a pre-hackathon event on 15th March to educate participating teams on Dialog’s NB-IoT network, special device packages, IoT developer kits and the future of IoT in Sri Lanka, and phase two being the hackathon itself, held on the 17th of March at the Dialog Head Office Auditorium.

HackaDev-2017

by: UNDP

(National Youth Software Competition)

UNDP together with their 12 sustainable goals have initiated a youth software competition inorder to get the youth of the nation to build the world a better place with the newest technology. The task assigned was to find a solution for a problem in the real world context which satisfy the 12 main sustainable goals of UNDP.

We team 'Spark-X' introduced an app which will encourage people to manage waste efficiently. Obviously it will help related authorities and general people by giving information on recycling, reusing & reducing wastages. App will locate users' location & match it with the municipal council workers, particular municipal council's location and it will show the nearest workers to that location. Then people can post the photo of garbage, an alert will be sent to that particular worker and the responsible municipal council/parties through the app as an SMS asking them to attend that location and get remove the garbage.

Biz-Minds

by: Young Enterprenure's Club of University of Kelaniya

The Inter-University Business Plan Competition (Biz Minds 2017) was held on the 5th of July 2017 at A. T. H. Sunil Shantha Memorial Auditorium of the Faculty of Commerce and Management Studies, University of Kelaniya. This competition was organized by the Young Entrepreneur’s Association of the Department of Commerce & Financial Management, University of Kelaniya.

The purpose of this competition was to animate the future dream business ideas of the Undergraduates. The keynote speech on “Start Improve Your Business” was delivered by Prof. Thilakasiri, the Director General of the Sri Lanka Institute of Development Administration (ASLIDA). The candidates representing the University of Kelaniya, the University of Moratuwa and the University of Sri Jayawardenepura participated in this competition. Out of 74 teams, We (team 'SparkX') were selected for the semi-finals and We presented an online platform between street vendors in Pettah and customers to order any desired product from any of Pettah shops.
